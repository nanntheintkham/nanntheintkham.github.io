My Favorite food

I would say I like eating. If I have to describe my palatte, I like food that are spicy and sour. I don't really prefer very sweet things.
Below are the list of some of my favorite food.

1. Mohinga

Mohinga is a rice noodle and fish soup from Myanmar and an essential part of Burmese cuisine, considered by many to be the national dish of Myanmar.

2. Dumpling

Dumpling is a broad class of dishes that consist of pieces of cooked dough (made from a variety of starch sources), often wrapped around a filling.

3. Fruits

They're my favorite snack. I like strawberry the most if I have to choose. They're healthy too. I always eat fruits almost everyday.

4. Mala Xian Guo

Mala xiang guo, roughly translated into English as "spicy stir-fry hot pot", is a Chinese dish prepared by stir frying. Strongly flavored with mala, it often contains meat and vegetables, and has a salty and spicy taste.
