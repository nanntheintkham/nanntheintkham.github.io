Myanmar(Burma)

Myanmar, officially the Republic of the Union of Myanmar, also known as Burma (the official name until 1989), is a country in Southeast Asia.
More than two years since the February 2021 military coup in Myanmar, the junta’s atrocities continue to escalate, driving the country further into a humanitarian and human rights catastrophe. The junta’s widespread and systematic abuses – including extrajudicial killings, torture, and indiscriminate attacks on civilians – amount to crimes against humanity and war crimes.
